\section{Failure Analysis Case Studies (AI Engine)}
One of the important things to note about Nebula is the accuracy of the failure analysis engine. Here are three examples.

\subsection{Case Study 1: Missing IAM Permissions}
While trying to deploy the S3 bucket within the constrained region using Terraform, it was shown as “Access Denied.” The AI Copilot checked the logs and correctly figured out that the IAM user didn't have the required S3:CreateBucket permission in the particular VPC.

\subsection{Case Study 2: Provider Quota Limits}
In another case, while testing the bulk provisioning (TC-12), Azure showed "Quota Exceeded." The AI successfully understood the problem and suggested increasing the quota in the Azure portal.
