\begin{abstract}
As enterprise infrastructure scales across heterogeneous cloud environments, the complexity of managing multi-cloud ecosystems (AWS, Azure, GCP) introduces significant operational overhead and vendor lock-in risks. This paper presents \textit{Cloud Simplify}, a next-generation SaaS orchestration platform designed to unify multi-cloud provisioning through a reactive Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) engine. By integrating FastAPI, Celery, and Terraform, the platform decouples infrastructure logic from provider-specific APIs, enabling seamless, high-performance resource deployment. Furthermore, the paper explores the integration of intelligent features for predictive cost analysis and strategic risk mitigation, laying the foundation for autonomous cloud management. Our implementation demonstrates a reduction in provisioning latency and enhanced security through zero-trust credential encapsulation, aligning with NIST and ISO/IEC standards for cloud portability.
\end{abstract}
